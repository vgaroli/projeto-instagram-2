Dogs, what lovely animals they are, and some of them, just like us, need help. It's been getting cold outside, and shelters are struggling to keep all of them fed. This is why we, the Global Program high school students, decided to help them with community service. We already collected a bunch of bottle caps, and now we are raising donations of cat and dog food. But wait, where are we supposed to deliver their food? You can find the boxes in the reception, or in the coordination, or even in the teacher's room. It depends on your school, the dogs are counting on you.
